# Wikify Style Guide

Write neutral encyclopedia prose. The reader should see a finished wiki
page, not the workflow that produced it.

## Prose

- Active voice where natural.
- One concept per sentence.
- Prefer concrete numbers over vague quantifiers.
- Keep terminology consistent.
- Use short, direct sentences for definitions.
- Distinguish observation, inference, and speculation.

## Hard Bans

- No `[[wikilinks]]` in body prose.
- No em dashes as parenthetical separators.
- No generation meta-commentary.
- No methodology disclosure.
- No phrases such as "in this corpus", "appears in this corpus", "this
  corpus contains", "in this article", or "as discussed above".
- No first-person research voice such as "we examine", "we show", or
  "our analysis".

## Citation Voice

Weave evidence into prose. Cite factual claims with `[^eN]` markers.
Do not cite a source for claims the supplied quote does not support.
