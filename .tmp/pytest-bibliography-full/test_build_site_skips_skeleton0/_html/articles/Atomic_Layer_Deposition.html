Definition

ALD is a self-limiting vapor-phase thin-film growth technique.

Mechanism / Process

ALD proceeds through pulsed half-reactions1. Each pulse saturates the surface1. The cycle repeats to grow films1.

Key Facts

  • ALD produces conformal films1.
  • Process temperatures are mild1.
  • Growth is sub-monolayer per cycle1.

In This Corpus

The corpus discusses ALD as a fabrication step1. Multiple sources cite its conformality1.
